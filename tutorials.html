Flower Keychain üå∏

1. Start with a magic ring.
2. Crochet 6 single crochets into the ring.
3. Chain 3, then double crochet into the same stitch to form petals.
4. Repeat around to make 6 petals.
5. Attach a keychain ring with yarn.

Heart Keychain ‚ù§Ô∏è

1. Chain 6 and slip stitch to form a loop.
2. Work 12 double crochets into the loop.
3. Shape into a heart by alternating half-double and double crochets.
4. Fasten off and attach to a keychain ring.

Fruit Keychain üçì

1. Start with a magic ring and 6 single crochets.
2. Increase each round until you form a small ball.
3. Use red yarn for the fruit body and green yarn for leaves.
4. Stuff lightly and close the top.
5. Attach to a keychain ring.
